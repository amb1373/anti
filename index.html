About Dr. Beshkoideh

International Expertise, Exceptional Results

Dr. Nader Beshkoideh is a general and cosmetic surgeon in Tehran, graduated from the University of Ljubljana, Slovenia, and holds a GMC board certification in the UK. He began his professional career in therapeutic and cosmetic surgeries in 2006 with extensive surgical experience in hospitals across Slovenia, England, and Iran.

He is a member of the European Society of Surgeons and consistently uses the latest techniques by participating in international surgical congresses and specialized courses in modern surgical methods.
